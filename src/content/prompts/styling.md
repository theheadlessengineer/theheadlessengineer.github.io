# **Prompt Set: Identity-Preserved Fashion Transformation**

## **1. Prompt: Single Male Transformation**

**Task:** Retain the subject's exact facial identity while upgrading the outfit and image quality to high-end fashion standards.

**1. Identity & Face (CRITICAL):**

* **Facial Features:** **Strictly do not alter** the subject’s nose, eyes, mouth, or bone structure. The person must remain instantly recognizable.
* **Skin:** Apply subtle skin smoothing (beautification) to remove blemishes, but preserve natural skin tone and shadows. Do not airbrush to the point of looking plastic.
* **Beard/Facial Hair:**
* *If the source image has a beard:* Keep the original shape but make the hair texture smoother and sharper.
* *If the source image is clean-shaven:* **Keep it clean-shaven.** Do not add a beard.



**2. Attire (Luxury & Elegant):**

* **Outfit:** Change current clothing to a high-quality **Wool Blend, Linen, or Thick Cloth Suit**.
* **Styling:** Pair with a high-quality button-down shirt and a **Silk Ascot Scarf** (tucked in).
* **Color:** Choose a luxury color palette (Charcoal, Navy, Deep Brown, or Slate Grey) that fits the lighting.
* **Accessories:** Metal luxury watch (Black or Silver, **no gold**).

**3. Physique & Posture:**

* **Body:** Adjust the suit fit to suggest wide shoulders and strong arms (masculine fit) without distorting the head-to-body ratio.
* **Pose:** Maintain the original pose (e.g., leaning/standing) but ensure the posture looks confident.

**4. Scene:**

* **Background:** **Use the original photo's background.** You may apply a slight blur (bokeh) or enhance color vibrancy, but do not replace the location.

## **2. Prompt: Single Female Transformation**

**Task:** Retain the subject's exact facial identity while upgrading the outfit and image quality to high-end fashion standards.

**1. Identity & Face (CRITICAL):**

* **Facial Features:** **Strictly do not alter** the subject’s facial geometry or features.
* **Skin:** Apply "glass skin" smoothing—radiant and flawless but natural.
* **Makeup:** Enhance makeup slightly (polished look), but do not change the subject's natural aesthetic.

**2. Hair (Strict Adherence):**

* **Style:** **Keep the exact length and hairstyle** from the original photo.
* **Texture:** Upgrade the hair quality to look silky, shiny, and frizz-free.

**3. Attire (Luxury & Elegant):**

* **Outfit:** High-end fashion attire (Tailored blazer, silk blouse, or structured dress).
* **Texture:** Focus on rich fabrics like silk, velvet, or fine wool.
* **Jewelry:** Minimal, tasteful luxury accents.

**4. Scene:**

* **Background:** **Use the original photo's background.** Enhance contrast and color slightly for a polished look.

## **3. Prompt: Multi-Person Group Transformation**

**Task:** Upgrade the clothing and image quality for all subjects while keeping every person's face and hairstyle recognizable.

**1. Identity & Face (CRITICAL for All):**

* **Likeness:** **Strictly preserve the facial identity of every person** in the photo. Do not face-swap or morph features.
* **Skin:** Apply high-definition skin smoothing to all subjects.
* **Facial Hair (Men):** Respect the original state (Beard stays beard, shaved stays shaved). Only improve grooming/sharpness.
* **Hair (Women):** Maintain original length and style for all women; improve texture only.

**2. Male Attire (Varied Luxury):**

* **Theme:** Elegant Suit + Shirt + Ascot Scarf.
* **Variation:** **Do not use a uniform.** Assign distinct color palettes to each man (e.g., Man A in Charcoal suit/Maroon scarf, Man B in Navy suit/Grey scarf, Man C in Brown suit/Blue scarf).
* **Material:** Wool blend, Linen, or Thick Cloth textures.

**3. Female Attire (Varied Luxury):**

* **Theme:** Elegant Evening/Cocktail wear.
* **Variation:** Distinct outfits (Dresses or Suits) in jewel tones or neutrals that harmonize with the men but do not match them identically.

**4. Scene:**

* **Background:** **Keep the exact background from the input image.**
* **Atmosphere:** Enhance lighting to be warmer and more cinematic. Apply a slight blur to background elements to make the people pop.
